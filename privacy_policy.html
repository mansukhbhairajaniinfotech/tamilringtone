Privacy Policy

Tamil Ringtones App
Last Updated: April 18, 2026

Welcome to Tamil Ringtones (the “App”), developed and operated by Kashyap Infotech (“we”, “us”, or “our”). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy explains what information our App handles, how it is used, and the choices you have.

By downloading, installing, or using the App, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the App.

1. About the App

Tamil Ringtones is a free Android application that lets users browse, preview, and set Tamil movie ringtones, BGMs and love tones as their device ringtone, notification tone, or alarm tone. All ringtones are bundled inside the App package — no audio is uploaded from or to your device.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide Directly

We do not ask you to create an account, log in, or submit any personal information (such as name, phone number, email, or address) to use the App. The only time you share information directly with us is if you voluntarily email us for feedback or support — in that case we will receive your email address and the content of your message.

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

The App itself does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information from your device. However, certain technical and advertising-related data may be collected by integrated third-party services (see Section 4) for the purpose of serving advertisements and basic analytics. This may include:

  • Device type, model, manufacturer and operating system version
  • Language and approximate (country/region) location derived from IP address
  • Advertising Identifier (AAID) for ad personalization and frequency capping
  • App version, crash logs, and basic usage diagnostics
  • Network state (whether the device is online)

2.3 Locally Stored Files

When you choose to set a ringtone, the App copies the selected audio file from its bundled assets to your device’s local storage so that the Android system can use it as a ringtone. These files remain on your device only and are never transmitted to us or any third party.

3. Permissions We Request

The App requests only the permissions required for its core functionality:

Modify System Settings (WRITE_SETTINGS) Required to set the selected audio as your phone’s ringtone, notification or alarm tone. The App opens the system settings page so you can grant this permission yourself; we never bypass it.
Storage (READ / WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE) Required on older Android versions to save the ringtone file to a location that the system ringtone manager can access. We never read your personal files, photos, documents, or other media.
Internet (INTERNET) Required to load advertisements served by Google AdMob and to fetch our remote ad-configuration file that controls whether ads are enabled.
Advertising ID (AD_ID) Used by Google AdMob to deliver and measure advertisements. You can reset this identifier or opt out of personalized ads at any time from your device’s Settings → Google → Ads menu.

4. Third-Party Services

Google AdMob

We display advertisements through Google AdMob (a service provided by Google LLC) including App Open, Interstitial, Banner and Native ad formats. Google may collect and process certain information as described in their privacy policy and may use cookies or device identifiers to provide and personalize ads.

Read more: Google Privacy Policy  ·  AdMob & Privacy

Remote Configuration

The App fetches a small JSON file from a public GitHub repository to determine ad-display settings (whether ads are shown and at what frequency). This is a one-way download; no information about you or your device is sent during this request beyond the standard HTTP headers (such as IP address) handled by GitHub.

Google Play Services

The App relies on Google Play Services for ad delivery and standard Android functionality. Google’s data practices are governed by their own Privacy Policy.

5. How Information Is Used

The limited information described above is used solely to:

  • Operate the core features of the App (playing and setting ringtones)
  • Display advertisements that keep the App free for users
  • Maintain stability, fix bugs, and improve performance
  • Respond to user feedback or support requests sent by email

We do not sell, rent, or trade any user information to third parties for marketing purposes.

6. Children’s Privacy

The App is intended for general audiences and is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can promptly remove it. Advertising shown in the App is configured to comply with family-policy guidelines on the Google Play Store.

7. Data Security

Because the App does not collect personal information directly, there is minimal data for us to secure. For the third-party services used, we rely on the security mechanisms provided by those services (such as Google’s industry-standard encryption and infrastructure). However, no method of transmission over the Internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

8. Data Retention

We do not retain any personal data on our own servers. Files copied to your device (ringtone audio) remain there until you delete them or uninstall the App. Data handled by third-party services is retained according to their respective policies.

9. Your Choices & Rights

  • Opt out of personalized ads: Open Settings → Google → Ads on your device and enable “Opt out of Ads Personalization”.
  • Reset advertising ID: Available in the same Google Ads settings menu.
  • Revoke permissions: Manage at any time from Settings → Apps → Tamil Ringtones → Permissions.
  • Uninstall: You may uninstall the App at any time, which removes all locally stored data.
